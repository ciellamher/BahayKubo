Brazo de Mercedes

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour

Brazo de Mercedes is a light, fluffy, and sweet Filipino dessert roll made from soft meringue and filled with rich, creamy custard. It’s a delicate and delicious treat perfect for celebrations.

Ingredients

For the meringue:
8 egg whites
1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
1 cup sugar

For the custard filling:
8 egg yolks
1/2 cup condensed milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Equipment

Electric mixer
Baking tray
Parchment paper
Spatula
Mixing bowls

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Beat the egg whites with cream of tartar using an electric mixer until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form.
  3. Spread the meringue evenly onto the prepared baking tray and bake for 20-25 minutes until lightly golden.
  4. While the meringue is baking, make the custard by cooking the egg yolks and condensed milk over low heat, stirring constantly until thickened. Add vanilla extract and set aside to cool.
  5. Once the meringue is done, remove from the oven and allow it to cool. Carefully flip the meringue onto a clean sheet of parchment paper.
  6. Spread the cooled custard evenly over the meringue, then gently roll the meringue to form a log. Serve and enjoy!

Notes

Variations: You can add a sprinkle of powdered sugar on top of the roll for extra sweetness.
Storage: Store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Nutrition Info (per serving, based on 8 servings):

Calories: 250
Protein: 6g
Fat: 8g
Carbohydrates: 35g
Sugar: 30g
Sodium: 70mg
