Buko Juice

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 5 minutes

Buko juice is the refreshing water from young coconut. It's a popular beverage in the Philippines, known for its sweet and hydrating properties, often enjoyed chilled.

Ingredients

1 young coconut (buko)
Ice (optional)
Sugar (optional, to taste)

Equipment

Coconut knife or cleaver
Glass for serving

Instructions

  1. Using a coconut knife, carefully cut open the young coconut to expose the water inside.
  2. Pour the coconut water into a glass. You can scoop out the soft coconut meat if desired.
  3. Add ice for a refreshing chill, and sweeten with sugar if preferred.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy the natural sweetness of Buko Juice!

Notes

Variations: You can blend the coconut meat with the water for a thicker drink.
Storage: Best enjoyed fresh but can be refrigerated for up to 1 day.

Nutrition Info (per serving):

Calories: 50
Protein: 1g
Fat: 0g
Carbohydrates: 13g
Sugar: 5g
Sodium: 10mg
