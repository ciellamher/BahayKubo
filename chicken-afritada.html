Chicken Afritada

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour 5 minutes

Chicken Afritada is a hearty Filipino dish where chicken is simmered with tomato sauce, vegetables, and spices, making it a flavorful and comforting meal.

Ingredients

1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (250g) tomato sauce
2 potatoes, peeled and quartered
1 carrot, sliced
1 bell pepper, sliced
1 bay leaf
1 tablespoon fish sauce
Salt and pepper to taste

Equipment

Large pot
Knife and chopping board
Measuring cups and spoons

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© the garlic and onion until softened.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce, then add the bay leaf, fish sauce, and water to cover the chicken.
  5. Simmer for 25 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  6. Add the potatoes, carrots, and bell pepper. Simmer until the vegetables are cooked through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Notes

Variations: You can add peas or green beans for extra vegetables.
Storage: Chicken Afritada can be stored in the fridge for up to 3 days in an airtight container.

Nutrition Info (per serving, based on 6 servings):
