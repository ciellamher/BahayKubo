Okoy

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes

Okoy is a crispy Filipino fritter made from shrimp and vegetables, deep-fried to golden perfection and served with a tangy vinegar dipping sauce.

Ingredients

1 cup small shrimp, cleaned
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup cornstarch
1 small carrot, julienned
1 small onion, sliced
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
1/2 cup water
Oil for frying

Equipment

Mixing bowl
Frying pan
Tongs
Paper towels

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add water and mix until a smooth batter forms.
  3. Stir in the shrimp, carrots, and onions until evenly coated with the batter.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Spoon portions of the shrimp mixture into the hot oil and flatten slightly with the back of the spoon.
  6. Fry each Okoy until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve with vinegar dipping sauce and enjoy!

Notes

Variations: You can add squash or sweet potato to the batter for a twist.
Storage: Okoy is best served fresh, but it can be stored in the fridge and reheated before serving.

Nutrition Info (per serving, based on 4 servings):
