Pandesal

Prep Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour 50 minutes

Pandesal is a soft and slightly sweet Filipino bread roll that is a breakfast staple, perfect when paired with coffee or hot chocolate.

Ingredients

4 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup warm water
1/4 cup sugar
2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup breadcrumbs

Equipment

Mixing bowl
Baking tray
Measuring cups and spoons
Parchment paper
Clean kitchen towel

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5 minutes until the mixture becomes foamy.
  2. Add oil, flour, and salt. Mix until a dough forms, then knead for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a kitchen towel, and let it rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350째F (180째C). Punch down the dough, divide it into small portions, and shape into balls.
  5. Roll each ball in breadcrumbs and place on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  6. Let the dough rise again for 30 minutes, then bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy your fresh Pandesal with butter or jam!

Notes

Variations: You can fill Pandesal with cheese, ube, or chocolate for a twist.
Storage: Store Pandesal in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days or freeze for longer storage.

Nutrition Info (per serving, based on 12 servings):

Calories: 180
Protein: 4g
Fat: 5g
Carbohydrates: 30g
Fiber: 1g
Sugar: 4g
