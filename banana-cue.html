Banana Cue

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes

Banana Cue is a popular Filipino snack made with deep-fried bananas coated in caramelized sugar and skewered on sticks.

Ingredients

6 saba bananas, peeled
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup cooking oil
Bamboo skewers

Equipment

Frying pan
Tongs
Mixing bowl
Skewers

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the peeled saba bananas and fry for 2-3 minutes, turning occasionally.
  3. Sprinkle the brown sugar over the bananas and continue frying until the sugar melts and coats the bananas.
  4. Remove the bananas from the pan and skewer them on bamboo sticks.
  5. Let cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious Banana Cue!

Notes

Variations: You can also use plantains instead of saba bananas.
Storage: Best served fresh, but can be kept for a few hours at room temperature.

Nutrition Info (per serving, based on 6 servings):
