Calamansi Juice

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 5 minutes

Calamansi juice is a refreshing beverage made from the juice of calamansi (Philippine lime). Itâ€™s tangy and sweet, perfect for quenching thirst on hot days.

Ingredients

1/2 cup fresh calamansi juice
1 cup water
2-3 tablespoons sugar (to taste)
Ice (optional)

Equipment

Measuring cups
Mixing spoon
Glass for serving

Instructions

  1. In a glass, combine fresh calamansi juice and water.
  2. Add sugar to taste and stir well until dissolved.
  3. Add ice if desired, and serve chilled. Enjoy your refreshing Calamansi Juice!

Notes

Variations: You can add honey or agave syrup for a healthier sweetener.
Storage: Best enjoyed fresh, but can be refrigerated for up to 1 day.
