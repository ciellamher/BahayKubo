Tuba

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Fermentation Time: 1-2 days
Total Time: 1-2 days 5 minutes

Tuba is a traditional Filipino beverage made from the sap of coconut trees. It's a mildly alcoholic drink enjoyed fresh or fermented, with a sweet and tangy flavor.

Ingredients

1 liter coconut sap (freshly tapped)

Equipment

Fermentation container
Glass for serving

Instructions

  1. Collect fresh coconut sap in a clean fermentation container.
  2. Cover the container and let it sit at room temperature for 1-2 days to ferment.
  3. Once fermented, pour the Tuba into a glass and enjoy it fresh or chilled.

Notes

Variations: You can sweeten with sugar or mix with fruits for added flavor.
Storage: Best enjoyed fresh, but can be kept in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
