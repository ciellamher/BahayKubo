Sizzling Sisig

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 45 minutes

Sizzling sisig is a famous Filipino dish made from chopped pork, typically from the face and ears, seasoned with onions, chili peppers, and calamansi, served on a hot plate.

Ingredients

500g pork belly, boiled and chopped
1 onion, chopped
2-3 green chili peppers, chopped
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons calamansi juice
1 tablespoon mayonnaise (optional)
Salt and pepper to taste

Equipment

Frying pan
Sizzling plate
Mixing bowl

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine chopped pork, onion, chili peppers, soy sauce, calamansi juice, and mayonnaise (if using). Mix well.
  2. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and saut√© the mixture until heated through.
  3. Transfer to a sizzling plate and serve hot. Enjoy your Sizzling Sisig!

Notes

Variations: You can add an egg on top for added richness.
Storage: Best enjoyed fresh, but leftovers can be refrigerated for up to 2 days.
