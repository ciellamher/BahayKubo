Bulalo

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 3 hours
Total Time: 3 hours 20 minutes

Bulalo is a traditional Filipino beef marrow soup, made from slowly simmered beef shank with rich bone marrow. Served with vegetables like corn, potatoes, and leafy greens, it's perfect for cold days.

Ingredients

1.5 kg beef shank (with bone marrow)
8 cups water
2 corn cobs, sliced
2 medium potatoes, peeled and quartered
1 onion, quartered
1 tablespoon fish sauce
1 tablespoon peppercorns
1 bunch bok choy (or other leafy greens)
1 small cabbage, quartered
Salt and pepper to taste

Equipment

Large stockpot
Slotted spoon
Knife and chopping board
Measuring cups and spoons

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add the beef shank and water. Bring to a boil and let it simmer for 2.5 hours or until the meat is tender. Skim off any impurities or fat that rise to the surface.
  2. Add the onions, peppercorns, and fish sauce. Continue to simmer for another 30 minutes.
  3. Put in the corn and potatoes. Simmer for 15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the cabbage and bok choy during the last 5-10 minutes of cooking.
  5. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice, and enjoy the rich, marrowy broth!

Notes

Variations: You can add other vegetables like carrots or green beans based on preference.
Storage: Bulalo can be stored in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2-3 days.

Nutrition Info (per serving, based on 6 servings):

Calories: 500
Protein: 35g
Fat: 38g
Carbohydrates: 18g
Fiber: 4g
Sugar: 3g
Sodium: 600mg
